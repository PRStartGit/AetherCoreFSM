1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Zynthio ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at zynthio.com and use our platform. This policy is part of our Privacy Policy.

By using Zynthio, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please adjust your browser settings or discontinue use of the Service.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track your activity, and provide a better user experience.

Cookies can be:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by Zynthio directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services we use (e.g., analytics, payment providers)

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: To enable core features like login, authentication, and security
  • Performance: To understand how you use our platform and identify areas for improvement
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: To analyze traffic, usage patterns, and user behavior
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats

Unlike cookies, local storage data is not sent to our servers with every request. It remains on your device until you clear your browser data.

9. How to Manage Cookies

9.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

How to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions
Important: Blocking or deleting essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or using key features of Zynthio.

9.2 Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics: Browser add-on
  • Do Not Track: Enable in your browser settings (not all websites honor this)

9.3 Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can:

  • iOS: Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies
  • Android: Chrome > Settings > Site settings > Cookies
