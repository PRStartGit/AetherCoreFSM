Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How does site-based pricing work?
You pay per physical location (site). For example, if you have 5 restaurants, you'd pay 5 × £10 = £50/month on the Pro plan. Unlimited users per site included.

What counts as a 'site'?
Any physical location where safety checks are performed. Each restaurant, pub, cafe, or kitchen is one site.

Can I change plans later?
Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card, debit card, or Direct Debit (annual plans). All processed securely via Stripe.

Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees, no hidden costs. Just the monthly per-site price.

What happens after the free trial?
You'll be charged for your first month. Cancel anytime before the trial ends - no charge, no questions asked.
